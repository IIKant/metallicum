Services

Our metallurgical plant provides metal and finished metal products to industries such as heavy and precision engineering, the construction industry, and the defense industry. The enterprise has four main metallurgical stages: smelting, rolling, drawing, finishing section. The plant's products occupy a stable position in the market and have established themselves as high-tech and high-quality.

SMOOTH PROFILE REINFORCEMENT

Designed for use in the reinforcement of prestressed reinforced concrete structures. Reinforcing bars are made from unalloyed and alloyed steel.

REINFORCING STEEL OF PERIODIC PROFILE

Designed for use in the reinforcement of precast concrete structures and in the construction of monolithic reinforced concrete.

HOT-ROLLED STEEL ANGLE

Used in almost all industries, it is especially widely used in the construction industry as rigid reinforcement for strengthening concrete in monolithic structures.

HOT-ROLLED STEEL CHANNEL WITH SLOPE

It is used for the construction and repair of buildings, as elements of supports and columns, for the construction of bridges and complex reinforced concrete structures.

Production

3D printer

3D printers for metal, we use for small-scale production. We recycle the metal powder, after each print, the unused powder is collected and sifted, and then topped up with fresh material to the level required for the next production. Since the cost of metal printing is very high, software simulators are often used to predict how a part will behave during processing. These topology optimization algorithms are used in other ways not only to increase mechanical performance and create lightweight parts.

Bolt production

The main task of these fasteners is to provide a reliable connection, the ability to assemble and disassemble structures. The nut with a washer is used in construction, mechanical engineering, furniture production and others. A washer is used to protect the surface that is being bolted together.

Production of high strength bolts

We produce high-strength bolts from alloy or carbon steel, the carbon content of which does not exceed 0.5%. These components are heat treated in a furnace. We apply the thread on special devices - automatic machines. The manufacture of high-strength bolts is a very relevant process today, because the scope of use of these hardware is large: mechanical engineering, construction, in the construction of railways, in the construction of bridges, and the connection of various structures.

Bearing production

Bearings are technical devices that are part of the bearings of rotating axles and shafts. They perceive the radial and axial loads applied to the shaft or axle and transfer them to the frame, housing or other parts of the structure. At the same time, they must also hold the shaft in space, provide rotation, swing or linear movement with minimal energy loss. The efficiency, performance and durability of the machine largely depend on the quality of the bearings.

Production of certain components to order

We offer for delivery metal products in the form of: wire, tape, rod. It is possible to manufacture other sizes, according to the requirements of the client. It specializes in the production of metal products from stainless welding, heat-resistant, heat-resistant, corrosion-resistant and precision alloys. The company ensures the stability and reliability of production at the level of a large plant and at the same time a flexible approach to customers, the ability to work not only with in-line, but also with high-tech orders.
